About Cardiology

The Department of Cardiology at Medicare Hospital has been recently upgraded and equipped with advanced , state- of -the -art equipments. It provides relief to patients suffering from a wide variety of cardiac ailments. The focus of the treatment is particularly on interventional procedures. A team of excellent & experienced cardiologists around the clock available for cardiac emergiencies.
